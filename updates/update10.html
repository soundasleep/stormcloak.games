New art and tutorials

Since our last release, we've added lots of new art to the game. We've redone all of the solid elements (dirt, rock, ice, coal, etc.) with new painted textures, and have cleaned up the element edges.

We've completely redesigned the crashed ship at the start of the game, with beautiful new art and a new base entrance.

Finally, we've also added a new space background with parallax that is gorgeous and adds a lot more visual depth 🥰
Pawn conversations

Pawns now have a “social” level, and talk with each other when their social levels are low. These appear as little speech bubbles in-game, and the text (and replies) are based on their personality types, their traits and buffs, and what’s been happening in game.

The framework is designed to be flexible, and is going to be extended in future updates with new traits, buffs, events, and replies. (I think someone mentioned sleep-talking...?)
