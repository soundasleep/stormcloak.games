We're also thrilled to share that Adaptory is featured
in the [**Nonbinary Coded Games**](https://store.steampowered.com/curator/46105077/sale/NonbinaryCodedGames) festival launching today!
[Jevon](/team) is proudly non-binary, and strongly believes
everyone in the world should feel safe and supported to be their authentic selves.
Did you know that in Adaptory your crew can be non-binary too?

> July 14th is International Nonbinary People's Day, so we're showcasing games by devs who are nonbinary, genderqueer, and outside the gender binary, along with games that have nonbinary rep! Take a look and see the spectrum of what we have to offer 🌈

Please come and check out [some amazing games and free demos](https://store.steampowered.com/curator/46105077/sale/NonbinaryCodedGames),
