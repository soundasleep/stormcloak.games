Wow.

Wow!! I don't really have words – the last 48 hours have been amazing!

Pressing the big green "release" button was scary, but it's been super smooth since then – thank you, community testers!
As a team and on our Discord, we've been celebrating all of the milestones and achievements
that Adaptory has hit over the last 48 hours:
hitting #2 in "Popular Upcoming" on Steam, and briefly being in Steam's "Top Sellers";
hitting our first ten Steam reviews;
wishlists increasing by 10% overnight, and now [blasing past 20k](https://bsky.app/profile/stormcloak.games/post/3mdjujhuwv22m) 😮;
and at one point, there were 600 people watching Adaptory on Twitch! 🎉

I'm glad that people are playing the game, experimenting and learning, getting invested in their crew, discovering and having fun
(right now, [50!! people are playing!](https://steamdb.info/app/2201620/charts/)). And nobody's found all of Adaptory's secrets yet :p

I've just pushed the latest patch, [0.16.14](https://steamcommunity.com/games/2201620/announcements/detail/538877446766723489),
which fixes some very rare crashes, polishes some text and CHARLI steps, and also makes the breathability overlay easier to read:

- In the breathability overlay, very low levels of oxygen (less than 200g) are now shaded red
- Increased the maximum length of a tantrum from 0.1 days to 0.2 days
- Increased length of "visited ruins" buff (+10% happiness) for archaeologists from 0.25 days to 2 days
- Fixed explorers occasionally getting stuck in an infinite loop if they were trying to pick up an item that had moved
- Fixed the gas or liquid filter dropdown not working if UI scale was set to 110% or greater
- Fixed a few rare crashes from explorers writing diary entries
- Fixed some demo save files not being loadable in the full game
- And more: [Check out the patch notes here](https://steamcommunity.com/games/2201620/announcements/detail/538877446766723489)

I'm still trying to find any last remaining bugs or crashes, or if anything is confusing.
Please let me know! We have a new [bug reporting channel in our Discord](https://adaptorygame.com/discord),
and you can always send me a DM or [email](mailto:jevon@stormcloak.games) too.

**Thank you** to everyone who's joined us in this journey, have bought a copy of Adaptory, and have left a review.
If you are enjoying the game, please consider **leaving an [honest review on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2201620/Adaptory/)**.
Every single review helps a lot!!
