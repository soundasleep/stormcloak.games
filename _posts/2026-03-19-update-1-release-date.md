Time flies! It feels like we only just [entered Early Access](/2026/01/26/early-access-live), but we've been busy in the background working
on Adaptory's first EA update while simultaneously releasing [five small patches since release](https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/2201620).

We are very excited to share that we have an official launch date for Adaptory's first major Early Access update...

**Update 1: The Materials Update** is launching at **10am Friday 3 April** (PDT)!

- **New Zealand** – 6am NZDT (4 April)
- **Australia** – 4am AEDT / 1am AWST (4 April)
- **Germany** – 7pm CEST (3 April)
- **United Kingdom** – 5pm UTC / 6pm BST (3 April)
- **United States** – 10am PDT / 1pm EDT (3 April)

This is Adaptory's first major EA update, with many more to come, and it adds a lot of new content:
from **new ways to play the game**, to **new systems and buildings to explore**,
and of course **more polish**, tutorials, and **quality of life** improvements.
I [want to hear your feedback](/discord) on the new systems so we can continue to refine and improve Adaptory!

Over the next week we will share some previews of what's coming,
so make sure you're following us on
[BlueSky](https://bsky.app/profile/stormcloak.games),
[Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/stormcloakgames),
[Mastodon](https://mastodon.gamedev.place/@stormcloak), and [join us on Discord](/discord) –
and [wishlist the game on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2201620/Adaptory/) if you haven't done so already.

Also: Please consider [leaving an honest review of Adaptory on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2201620/Adaptory/),
if you haven't already done so. Now that Adaptory has launched into Early Access,
every single review helps a lot with visibility on Steam, and will be greatly appreciated. 🙂
