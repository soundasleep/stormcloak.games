After an amazing [Kiwi Game Zone 2025](https://www.nzgdc.com/kiwi-games-zone) and
[PAX Aus 2025](/2025/10/01/pax-aus-2025), we are thrilled to announce that we will be part of
the **Frosty Mini December 2025 showcase**, coming next month.

And we have some very, very exciting Adaptory news to share! So please come join us 😊

<figure class="image">
  <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@FrostyGamesFest"><img src="/assets/screenshots/2025-11-13-frosty-mini-2025.png"></a>
</figure>

**What:**<br>
[Frosty Mini](https://www.youtube.com/@FrostyGamesFest) is a half-hour showcase with exciting news and surprise
updates from 20+ games made in Australia and Aotearoa New Zealand.

**When:**<br>
10 December 2025, at 8am AEDT

**How to Watch Frosty Mini:**<br>
The first ever Frosty Mini will broadcast on December 10 AEDT (December 9 for our friends in the US).
Check the time zones below to find out when you can tune in worldwide:

- **Australia** – 8:00am AEDT \| 5:00am AWST (10 December)
- **New Zealand** – 10:00am NZDT (10 December)
- **Japan** – 6:00am JST (10 December)
- **United States** – 1:00pm PT \| 4:00pm ET (9 December)
- **United Kingdom** – 9:00pm GMT (9 December)

Frosty Mini will be livestreamed via [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@FrostyGamesFest) and [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/frostygamesfest).
Post-show the VOD will also be available at [youtube.com/@FrostyGamesFest](https://www.youtube.com/@FrostyGamesFest).

We will also share the announcement afterwards on our
[Discord](/discord), [blog](/blog) (which you're reading now!),
[mailing list](/updates), and on our socials.
