Each playthrough of Adaptory is unique, thanks to both the explorer generation,
but also the way in which Adaptory procedurally generates every world.
The world generation process has been massively revamped and polished for Early Access,
with lots for your explorers to discover.

**(Spoilers ahead!)**

## Ancient civilisations

<figure class="image">
  <a href="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-ruins.png"><img src="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-ruins.png"></a>
  <figcaption>Discovering ancient civilisations deep underground</figcaption>
</figure>

As your crew digs deeper into the planetoid, they'll discover **mysterious ruins** –
brickwork, columns, vases, and statues – all seemingly abandoned by a previous civilisation.
Why are they here, and what happened to this planetoid's previous inhabitants?

## Springs

<figure class="image">
  <a href="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-springs.png"><img src="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-springs.png"></a>
  <figcaption>Naturally-generating springs provide an endless source of water</figcaption>
</figure>

Your explorers may also uncover a new world feature: **natural springs!** These are
permanent features that continually create and emit water into the world, allowing you to
set up more self-sufficient and sustainable bases.

There are even rumours of magma springs deep underground.

## Relics

<figure class="image">
  <a href="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-relic.png"><img src="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-relic.png"></a>
  <figcaption>What are these odd objects we've found?</figcaption>
</figure>

Deep underground, your explorers may also uncover **ancient relics** buried
in the ground. Jars, instruments, objects, and all sorts of other weird stuff have been left behind –
and your explorers love them. By using the brand new **Display Stand** building, you can also proudly
showcase your discoveries, giving nearby explorers a bonus to their mood.

## The story continues

<figure class="image">
  <a href="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-crashing-ship.png"><img src="/assets/screenshots/2026-01-26-crashing-ship.png"></a>
  <figcaption>Did someone pick up our distress signal?</figcaption>

Early Access continues the story where the demo left off. After powering up your distress transmitter, filling
it with transmitite, and sending out your distress signal,
it appears another ship **received your transmission** and has landed nearby. And without spoiling too
much – you'll probably want to investigate the ship sooner rather than later...
